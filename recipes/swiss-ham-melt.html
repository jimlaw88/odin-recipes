Swiss Ham Melt

Description

Smoked ham topped with melted gruyere and a spicy sauce, on an english muffin.

Ingredients

  • 1 x english muffin
  • 80g swiss cheese
  • 80g smoked ham
  • 10g sriracha
  • 15g light mayo

Directions

  1. Mix sriracha and mayo together to create sauce.
  2. Split muffin and spread sauce on each half
  3. Then layer sandwich with cheese, then ham, then cheese, then ham and finally cheese again
  4. Air fry sandwich for 12 minutes at 150 degrees celsius.
  5. Enjoy!
