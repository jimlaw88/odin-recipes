California Burrito

Description

Crsipy burrito filled with tender chicken, fries, melted cheddar topped with guacamole and yoghurt.

Ingredients

  • 1 burrito wrap
  • 100g chicken thighs
  • 100g fat free yoghurt
  • 50g guacamole
  • 80g frozen fries
  • 30g low fat cheddar

Directions

  1. preheat oven then cook fries as per package instructions.
  2. Cook chicken thighs for 15 minutes at 190 degrees in air fryer>
  3. Layer cooked chicken and fries on wrap
  4. Top with grated cheese, then yoghurt, then guacamole.
  5. Fold wrap, then place in the over for 2-4 minutes.
  6. Enjoy!
